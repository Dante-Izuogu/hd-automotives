About HD Automotives

HD Automotives is a trusted independent garage delivering high-quality vehicle repairs, diagnostics and servicing. We combine experience, attention to detail and honest pricing to ensure every customer leaves confident in the safety and performance of their vehicle.

Our Services

General Servicing

Our full and interim servicing packages include thorough inspections, fluid replacements, filter changes and performance checks to keep your vehicle running efficiently and reliably throughout the year.

Diagnostics

Using advanced diagnostic tools, we quickly identify engine faults, electrical issues and warning light problems — saving you time and preventing minor issues from becoming costly repairs.

MOT Preparation

Our pre-MOT inspections ensure your vehicle meets safety standards. We check brakes, suspension, emissions and lighting so you can pass your MOT with confidence.

Brakes & Suspension

We carry out essential brake pad, disc and suspension repairs to maintain safe handling and stopping performance. Safety is always our priority.

Tyre Services

From puncture repairs to full tyre replacements, we provide fast, reliable tyre services including balancing and pressure checks to ensure maximum grip, stability and fuel efficiency.
